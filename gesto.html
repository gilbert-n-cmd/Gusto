Our Story

Discover the passion behind our authentic Italian restaurant

Family Tradition Since 1985

Founded by the Rossi family in 1985, Gusto Italiano brings the authentic flavors of Southern Italy to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional methods that make Italian cuisine so special.

We source the finest ingredients from local producers and import specialty items directly from Italy. Our pasta is made fresh daily, and our sauces simmer for hours to develop rich, complex flavors.

At Gusto Italiano, we believe that dining is an experience to be savored. Our warm, inviting atmosphere and attentive service create the perfect setting for memorable meals with family and friends.

Marco Rossi
